# Boardoza TPS563203 5V Regulator Module

The **Boardoza TPS563203 5V Regulator Module** is a compact and efficient DC-DC buck (step-down) converter designed to provide a stable **5V output** from a wide **7V–17V DC input** range. It is based on the **Texas Instruments TPS563203** synchronous buck regulator, known for its high efficiency and low quiescent current.

This module is ideal for powering **microcontrollers, sensors, communication modules, and embedded systems** that require a reliable 5V supply from higher-voltage sources such as adapters, batteries, or industrial power rails. Its small form factor and robust thermal performance make it suitable for both prototyping and production use.

## Key Features

- **Wide Input Voltage Range:** Supports 7V to 17V DC input for flexible power sources.  
- **Stable 5V Output:** Provides a fixed and regulated 5V DC output suitable for logic and embedded systems.  
- **High Output Current Capability:** Delivers up to 2A continuous output current.  
- **High Efficiency Buck Topology:** Synchronous step-down conversion for reduced power loss.  
- **Low Quiescent Current:** Optimized for power-efficient designs with minimal standby consumption.  
- **Integrated Soft-Start:** Ensures controlled startup to protect connected loads.

## Technical Specifications

**Model:** TPS563203 5V  
**Manufacturer:** Boardoza  
**Manufacturer IC:** Texas Instruments  
**Regulator Type:** DC-DC Buck (Step-Down) Converter  
**Input Voltage:** 7V – 17V DC  
**Output Voltage:** 5V DC  
**Output Current:** Up to 2A  
**Switching Frequency:** 600 kHz  
**Quiescent Current:** 110 µA (typ.)  
**Soft-Start Time:** 1.4 ms (fixed)  
**Reference Voltage Accuracy:** ±1.5% (25°C), ±2% (–40°C to +125°C)  
**Operating Temperature:** –40°C to +125°C  
**Board Dimensions:** 20 mm × 40 mm
